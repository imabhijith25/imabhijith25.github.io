About Us

Helping the older generation for a better future

What we do is beyond borders.

We provide the neglected and the elderly members of the society with a helping hand that will allow them to improve their conditions. We have many volunteers who work diligently to supply unending sources of food and medicines for the people of lower strata. We also operate a number of old age homes that focus on providing love and care to the elderly thus providing them with a peaceful and comforting atmosphere. We are always in the lookout for volunteers and if you think that you can be of help then feel free to contact us at volunteers@nirvana.com
